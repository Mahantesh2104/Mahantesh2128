The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional T20 cricket league that has garnered massive popularity since its inception in 2008. The league features some of the best cricketers from around the world, with teams from different cities across India competing for the prestigious IPL title. Teams like Chennai Super Kings (CSK), Mumbai Indians (MI), Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), and Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are some of the most well-known in the league, each with its own fan base.
