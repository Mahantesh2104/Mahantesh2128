History of Cricket

Cricket, as we know it today, originated in England during the late 16th century. Initially played as a children's game, it evolved into an organized sport by the 17th century. The first recorded match took place in 1646, and by the 18th century, cricket had become England's national sport. With the expansion of the British Empire, cricket spread globally. Test cricket began in 1877, followed by One Day Internationals in 1971, and the revolutionary Twenty20 format in 2003. Today, cricket is a unifying force that brings together people across nations, celebrating their love for the game.
